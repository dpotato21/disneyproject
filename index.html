What Is Disney?

In case you haven't heard, Disney is the multi billion dollar company that has been providing entertainment for years. However, Disney is not only one company. Over time, it has transformed into many different networks, and it now has control over many different popular brands today. Some may be surprising...


The Beginning History of Disney

Many people know that Disney was originally started by Walt Disney. In 1923, Walt and his brother Roy began the company. Contrary to popular belief, Mickey Mouse was not Walt's first creation. He actually started off creating Alice's Wonderland, which was about a young girl in an animated world. These went on to be known as Alice Comedies. After a contract mishap regarding the rights to Alice Comedies, Walt went on to create a new character. This character would end up being one of the most iconic symbols of the entertainment indusry. After three attempts, Mickey Mouse was born.


After nearly a century, Disney has showed no signs of slowing down. The company has purchased so many other corporations that a large portion of the entertainment we watch today is owned by Disney. This website will try and break down the different parts of Disney, as well as talk about different pieces of entertainment you may not have known were actually a part of Disney.
